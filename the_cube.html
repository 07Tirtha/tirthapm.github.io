The Cube

The Cube is a 3D game that I made in Unity. It is my very first 3d game. The rules are simple- You control a cube by using the arrow keys or the wasd keys and try to avoid the obstacles. The obstacles are randomly generated. The obstacles will become more difficult as the game goes on along with introducing new kinds of blocks. You can play level mode or endless runner mode and try to beat your highscore.
